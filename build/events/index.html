Our Major Events

This year the Society is working harder than ever before to bring new events, along with some other classics, to the student population.

LAN Parties

LAN Parties are a favourite amongst students in the Department. Mental health is important and this event serves an opportunity for students to relax for a night of games during the typically hectic midterm season. Come out and form new friendships with people in all years over a game of Super Smash Bros!

Hack-a-thons

The Society is currently partnering with ESS to coordinate and run various code retreats and hack-a-thons during the coming school year. Regardless of your technical skill-level there will be something for everyone to participate in.

Check hackyourlearning.ca for announcements and updates about events! There will be plenty of opportunities planned for students to pratice before the major competition.

Technical Elective Night

Tech Elec Night is an opportunity for students to learn about the elective courses available during the next school year. Attend the event to hear from the professors about the content you will see and the sort of coursework to expect. Doubts about enrolling in a course? Well, this is the perfect opportunity to ask a clarifying question before you potentially find yourself regretting your course choices!

Career Fair

Thinking about where to apply for internship or after graduation? A Career Fair is a perfect opportunity to learn what is available and make some valuable connections that can help you in the future. Be prepared with your resume and proper attire as you never know what may come from your interactions with the various employeers and recruiters!

Canadian University Software Engineering Conference

This year the Society is working to coordinate a trip to the Canadian University Software Engineering Conference, for those unaware CUSEC, as described by their site, is:

[...] an annual software engineering conference organized for students by students. It was founded in 2002 by a small team of tech enthusiasts on a mission to educate and expose students to a diverse range of areas in software engineering and computer science. The conference enables attendees to discover knowledgeable speakers, connect with sponsoring companies, and make lifelong friends, all in a safe and comfortable space.
