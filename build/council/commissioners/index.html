Coordinates with the VP Events to plan and host academic-oriented events.
Artistic Commissioner:
Create and distribute posters for Society's events.

Frosh/Engg Week Director:
Direct the Society's involvement in Frosh and Engg Week.

Assist with the maintenance of Society technologies, such as the webserver, Council Emails, and file server.
Commissioners: Ines Rosito
Operations Commissioner:

Professional Development Commissioner:
Coordinate with the VP Events to plan and host professional development events.
