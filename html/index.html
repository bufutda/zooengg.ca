The Electrical, Software, and Computer Engineering Students' Society at the University of Calgary is an organization committed to providing students with a variety of academic and social events to enrich their experience at university and throughout their degree. We strive to represent Electrical, Computer, and Software engineering students at the University of Calgary within the campus, to other universities, and to the community at large.

As well as the Electrical, Computer, and Software Engineering Students Society, our organization also goes by the name ZOO. Any enquiries for the department of Electrical, Computer, and Software Engineering should be directed to relevant department employee. A list of contacts for the department can be found here.

Any enquiries to ZOO should either be voiced in-person in the homeroom (ICT 215), or via email to president@zooengg.ca. Questions or comments regarding this site should be directed via email to webmaster@zooengg.ca

ZOO abides by the Club Constitution (Updated March, 2016).
